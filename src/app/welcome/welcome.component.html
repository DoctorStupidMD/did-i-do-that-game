Did I do that!?

Did you do the thing? Say whatever it takes to prove your innocence... even if you're not so innocent.

How to play:

  1. Before the game starts, all players have provided three weird or quirky facts about themselves.
  2. Your goal is to select the connect the correct player to each "Did I do that?" prompt. Ask questions, accuse each other, throw someone under the bus.
  3. If the prompt belongs to you, fiercely defend and deny it - even if it's your own prompt! Throw them off your trail!
  4. Everyone will have 60 seconds per prompt to accuse, oppose, challenge, defend, rebuke, and just flat-out argue their guesses before locking in their selection.
  5. The player(s) who has matched the most prompts wins!

And remember, everyone is an enemy. Good luck and let's play!
